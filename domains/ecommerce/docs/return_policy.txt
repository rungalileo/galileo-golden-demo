RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase date. Items must be in unused condition with original packaging and tags attached.

Eligible for Return:
- Unused items in original packaging
- Items with original tags and labels
- Items purchased within the last 30 days
- Items with proof of purchase (receipt or order confirmation)

Not Eligible for Return:
- Used or opened consumable items (food, beverages, personal care)
- Items without original packaging
- Items damaged by customer
- Items purchased more than 30 days ago
- Final sale items (clearly marked)

Return Process:
1. Contact customer service to initiate return
2. Receive return authorization number (RMA)
3. Package item securely in original packaging
4. Ship to return address provided with RMA
5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days after receipt

Refund Method:
- Original payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- Processing time: 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item was defective or incorrect

Exchanges:
- Exchanges available for different sizes or colors (subject to availability)
- Must be requested within 30 days of purchase
- Customer responsible for return shipping costs

Defective or Incorrect Items:
- Full refund including original shipping costs
- Return shipping provided at no cost to customer
- Priority processing for refund

Special Circumstances:
- Holiday purchases: Extended return window until January 31st
- Gift purchases: Can be returned with gift receipt within 30 days
