PAYMENT AND BILLING POLICY

ACCEPTED PAYMENT METHODS

Credit and Debit Cards:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- All major credit and debit cards accepted
- Cards must have valid expiration date and CVV code
- International cards accepted (may incur currency conversion fees)

Digital Wallets:
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Amazon Pay

Alternative Payment:
- Store credit and gift cards
- Buy now, pay later options (subject to approval)

PAYMENT PROCESSING

Authorization:
- Payment is authorized at time of order placement
- Funds are held but not charged until order ships
- Authorization may appear as pending charge on your statement

Charging:
- Your payment method is charged when your order ships
- You will receive email confirmation when payment is processed
- If payment fails, you will be notified and order may be cancelled

Currency:
- All prices displayed in USD (United States Dollar)
- International orders may be subject to currency conversion
- Exchange rates determined by your financial institution

BILLING INFORMATION

Billing Address:
- Must match the address on file with your payment method
- Required for credit card verification
- Can be different from shipping address

Invoice:
- Digital invoice provided via email upon order confirmation
- Includes itemized list of products, quantities, prices, and taxes
- Available in your account order history

Taxes:
- Sales tax applied based on shipping address
- Tax rates vary by state and local jurisdiction
- Tax amount calculated at checkout and shown before payment

PRICING AND DISCOUNTS

Price Guarantee:
- Prices are subject to change without notice
- Price at time of order is locked in
- We match competitor prices (see price match policy)

Discounts and Promotions:
- Promo codes must be entered at checkout
- Only one promo code per order
- Discounts cannot be combined unless otherwise stated
- Sale prices are final and cannot be combined with other offers

Price Match:
- We will match competitor prices on identical items
- Must be requested within 7 days of purchase
- Requires proof of lower price (advertisement or website)
- Excludes clearance, closeout, and limited-time promotions

REFUNDS AND CREDITS

Refund Processing:
- Refunds processed to original payment method
- Processing time: 5-7 business days after return is received
- Credit card refunds may take 1-2 billing cycles to appear
- PayPal refunds typically appear within 3-5 business days

Store Credit:
- Available for returns without receipt
- Issued as account credit
- Never expires
- Can be used for future purchases

Partial Refunds:
- Available for partial order returns
- Shipping costs refunded only if entire order is returned
- Restocking fees may apply to certain items (clearly marked)

BILLING DISPUTES

Dispute Resolution:
- Contact customer service within 30 days of charge
- Provide order number and description of issue
- We will investigate and respond within 5 business days

Unauthorized Charges:
- Report immediately to customer service and your bank
- We will investigate and provide documentation
- Full cooperation with financial institution fraud departments

Billing Errors:
- Contact us immediately if you notice billing errors
- We will correct errors and issue refunds if applicable
- Keep all receipts and order confirmations

SECURITY

Payment Security:
- All transactions encrypted using SSL/TLS technology
- We do not store full credit card numbers
- Payment information processed through PCI-compliant processors
- Your financial data is never shared with third parties

Privacy:
- Payment information used solely for transaction processing
- See our Privacy Policy for complete details
- We comply with all applicable data protection regulations

SUBSCRIPTIONS AND RECURRING PAYMENTS

Auto-Renewal:
- Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled
- You will be charged at the beginning of each billing cycle
- Email notifications sent before each renewal

Cancellation:
- Cancel anytime from your account settings
- Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
- No refunds for partial billing periods

Payment Failure:
- If payment fails, subscription may be suspended
- You will be notified and given opportunity to update payment method
- Service resumes once payment is successfully processed

INTERNATIONAL ORDERS

Currency Conversion:
- Prices displayed in USD
- Your bank may charge currency conversion fees
- Exchange rates determined by your financial institution

Customs and Duties:
- International orders may be subject to customs fees
- Customer responsible for all import duties and taxes
- Fees vary by country and are not included in order total

Payment Methods:
- International credit cards accepted
- PayPal available for most countries
- Some payment methods may not be available in all regions
